Williams, Stereo. Imagine No More Pandering: Why John Lennon's Protest Perennial Became an Anthem For the Clueless. 5 June 2020, www.billboard.com/articles/columns/rock/9396825/john-lennon-imagine-bill-de-blasio-anthem-clueless/.
This article sheds light on a recent event in New York City where supporter to defund the police used the song as a tool for unification. The article explains a brief history of the song, and ultimately explains how the message has been distorted by people who have little context about world institutions to sound like the perfect solution to any problem. This quote explains this more:
“Not only has the song become a go-to anthem for the detached, a song that asks that we imagine a world with no differences as opposed to challenging us to see each other fully but without hate, but it has also been distorted here for the purpose of squelching concerns about law enforcement institutions that are very likely irrevocably broken”
